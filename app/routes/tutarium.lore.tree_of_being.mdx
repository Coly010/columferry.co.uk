### What is it?
	- An enormous **Blackwood Tree**, with striking blue leaves.
	- Said to be imbued with **The Essence** and the source of the **Pocket of The Essence** in **Cealyn**
	- It has a grand brick tree surround
### Where is it?
	- Cealyn

### Background
	- The Tree of Being grew naturally.
	- No-one knows when, it seems to have always existed
	- It never loses its leaves, but any leaves that are removed grow back quickly
	- Leaves are collected from it at the end of Spring, during the **Tree Ceremony of Cealyn**
	- The leaves have been used in medicinal potions and remedies for centuaries
	- It is revered in **Cealyn**
